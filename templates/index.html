Send A Preview

Listen to previews of any artist over the phone

SAP is a simple way to listen to track previews over the phone. Just text an artist name to the following number, and we'll call you with 5 samples from their top-tracks.

+1 347-329-3425

Pretty useful when you're lost in that record store with no one to help and a poor 3G connection, right?

SAP is an experiment using APIs from Twilio and 7Digital together with Flask and Toast. You can read more here or check the source (and submit bug reports) on GitHub.

If you like what you've seen, contact us or check seevl.fm.
